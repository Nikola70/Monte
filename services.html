Services

At Hotel Monte, we make your stay as comfortable, convenient, and enjoyable as possible. In winter, we offer a complimentary shuttle service to the nearby Shino ski lift, making it easy to hit the slopes. Guests can also rent high-quality ski and snowboard equipment directly from the hotel, so you are always ready for a day of adventure. For relaxation after an active day, unwind with a soothing massage or warm up in our sauna.


Year-round, you can enjoy our well-equipped game room, indoor pool, and mountain bike rentals for exploring the beautiful surroundings in summer. Our conference room provides the ideal space for meetings and corporate events, while our friendly staff is always ready to assist with any special requests. Whether you are here for adventure, business, or pure relaxation, Hotel Monte has everything you need for an unforgettable stay.
